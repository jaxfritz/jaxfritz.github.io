    Jacqueline Fritz is a designer and interactive producer living and working between St. Louis and NYC with her new pup, Frankie Noodles. After dancing professionally, she began a business exploring intersections of movement and design, and later moved to Paris. There she worked in digital culture and business intelligence, segwayed into creative direction, and is currently the Managing Producer, Americas at Antenna International. She loves anything visually oriented, leading design teams, and helping others reach their full potential.
