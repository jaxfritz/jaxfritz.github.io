Jacqueline Fritz is an interactive producer and designer living and working between St. Louis and NYC with her new pup, Frankie Noodles. After dancing professionally, she began a business, BodyxDesign, and later moved to Paris. There she worked in digital culture and business intelligence, has since segwayed into creative direction, and is currently the Managing Producer, Americas, at Antenna International. She loves anything visually oriented, leading design teams, and helping others reach their full potential.
